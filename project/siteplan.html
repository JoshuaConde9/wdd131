Site Name: Joshua Conde | Digital Art and Drawings

I chose this name because it clearly shows that the site is going to be about my artworks, emphasizing that what I do the most are digital artworks and drawings, instead of other art forms like painting, water colour, sculpture, etc.

Site Purpose:

This site will be both a personal gallery and a professional portfolio, created to display my work, document and show my progress, and provide a way for others to reach me.

Scenarios:

Where can I follow this artist to see his latest works?

How can I contact this artist in case I want to commission him?
